My ADHD Assistant delivers AI-powered productivity support directly to your phone via text message — no apps to open, no dashboards to navigate. Just send a text, like messaging a friend.

Who this is for

This service is designed for individuals who benefit from low-friction, always-available support for executive function and day-to-day task management — particularly those who find traditional productivity apps difficult to use consistently.

If you've tried every to-do app and still feel behind, My ADHD Assistant works with how your brain actually functions.

Opt-in & messaging

To use this service, you must be an enrolled subscriber. Enrollment requires your explicit consent to receive SMS messages. You will not receive any messages unless you have opted in through the My ADHD Assistant registration process.

To stop receiving messages at any time, text STOP to My ADHD Assistant. Any subscription will be cancelled, and you will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
